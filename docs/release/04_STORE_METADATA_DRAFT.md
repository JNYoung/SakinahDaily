### English

#### Short description

Prayer times and local reminders, calm and private.

#### Full description

Sakinah Daily is a calm prayer companion for everyday Muslim worship moments.
This release helps you check prayer times, keep local prayer reminders, choose a
manual or preset prayer location, and begin an optional short daily worship
session.

Built for a quieter daily rhythm:

- Prayer times for your selected manual or preset location.
- Local prayer reminders with explanation before notification permission.
- A short optional daily worship session for intention, Quran reflection, Dua,
  and Dhikr.
- Privacy Center and Delete local data controls.
- Arabic RTL, Bahasa Indonesia, and English app surfaces.
- No ads.
- No tracking.
- No GPS permission in this release.
- No AI fatwa or AI religious Q&A.
- No generic Quran TTS and no background music under Quran recitation.

Sakinah Daily is focused on reminders, calm worship routines, and privacy-first
local settings. It is not a religious authority, medical tool, social platform,
or Quran recitation correction service.

#### Keywords

Prayer, Muslim, Salah, Shalat, Reminder, Qibla, Sakinah, Ibadah, Dua, Dhikr
